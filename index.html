Intro

A short description of myself

Im a 28 years old, entrepeneur, techlover. Although it was not my profession, I believe that technology is a very important growing sector that can help improve people's lives. After the pandemic, I changed profession since I had the opportunity. I have spent two years working in the blockchain ecosystem while training as a fullstack developer.

In the meantime I have been working as a car mechanic for a multinational company. I have repaired every car model you can imagine, from a simple car to a Lamborghini. In 2019 I found myself in a situation that needed to be improved. My job cannot depend exclusively on going to a physical location. This together with the logic that has given me working with cars and knowing that everything has a process, made me decide to go into the world of programming and technology.

In 2020 I created my first website (which was a failure) but I learned a lot about wordpress development and when I saw that it was not so simple, I decided to train myself. At the end of 2020 I luckily joined a blockchain development academy and after the training they hired me and I worked with them for more than two years. While working with them I decided that I wanted to continue with it and so, I entered a training as a full stack programmer which I finished in July 2023. Since then, I have dedicated myself to help my environment to develop their projects, freelance work and to keep learning.
